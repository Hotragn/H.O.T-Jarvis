# Code of Conduct

Be decent. Specifically:

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions. Critique code, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Assume good faith; newcomers are welcome and "beginner" questions are fine.
- Keep discussions on-topic and public where possible.

Violations can be reported privately to the maintainer via GitHub. The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject contributions and, for repeated or severe violations, ban participants.

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1.
